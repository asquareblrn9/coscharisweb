About Coscharis Motors

More than cars.
A commitment to excellence.

Since 1977, Coscharis Motors PLC has been Nigeria's trusted partner in mobility. We represent five luxury and two value brands, delivering vehicles that inspire confidence and exceed expectations.

  • Ultra-modern showrooms across Nigeria
  • State-of-the-art aftersales facilities
  • Specialised & factory-trained technicians
  • Genuine parts, accessories & lifestyle solutions
  • Nationwide presence. Local expertise.
