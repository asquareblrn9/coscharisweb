About Coscharis Group

A legacy of excellence.
A future of impact.

Coscharis Group is a wholly owned Nigerian conglomerate with businesses and interests that span across key sectors of the economy. We are committed to delivering exceptional value, driving innovation and contributing to the progress of Africa.

Our Story

From humble beginnings to a diversified Pan-African conglomerate.

Founded in 1977 as a modest spare parts importation and distribution business, Coscharis Group has grown into one of Africa's most respected conglomerates.

What began as a commitment to quality and reliability has evolved into a diversified portfolio of businesses that serve millions of customers, create thousands of jobs, and contribute meaningfully to national development.

Guided by visionary leadership, strategic partnerships and a culture of excellence, we continue to build an institution that is timeless in its relevance.

Founder & President / CEO

Visionary leadership.
Enduring values.

Under the leadership of our Founder and President/CEO, Dr. Cosmas Maduka, CON, Coscharis Group has grown into a respected Pan-African brand, renowned for integrity, innovation and operational excellence.

His entrepreneurial spirit, business acumen and unwavering commitment to value creation continue to drive the Group's purpose and performance.

Our Vision

To build an institution that will be timeless in its relevance.

Our Mission

Coscharis will deliver excellent products and services to our customers, giving them the highest value for their money, and will contribute to societal development and progress by exploring and exploiting technological innovations, ploughing back resources for the society, providing fulfillment and a sense of pride in its employees; and will ensure the sustenance of these values by operating with increasing profit and waxing stronger and bigger into the future.
